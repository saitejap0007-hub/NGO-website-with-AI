Bridging the Digital Divide

We are dedicated to providing accessible tech education and digital tools to underprivileged youth, shaping the innovators of tomorrow.

Learn More

Our Mission

Founded on the belief that technology should be an equalizer, not a barrier, the Code for Good Foundation operates globally to bring mobile and web development resources to remote communities. From setting up community computer labs to providing mentorship in languages like Python and JavaScript, we empower the next generation to build their own futures.

What We Do

💻 Tech Bootcamps

Intensive, 8-week programs focusing on fundamental coding skills, UI design, and database management for high school students.

📱 Device Donation

We refurbish and distribute laptops and tablets to students who lack the hardware required to participate in modern education.

🤝 Mentorship Network

Connecting aspiring developers with industry professionals to guide them through portfolio building and internship acquisition.
